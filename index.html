Meet Dr. Chin

I am an Associate Professor at Biola University’s Rosemead School of Psychology and serve as an Associate Editor for the Journal of Psychology and Theology. My research focuses on improving access to care for diverse populations and on building and validating psychological measures. I frequently serve as the methodologist/statistician on externally funded projects and co‑lead Biola’s Culture and Mental Health Lab and Asian American Specialty Mental Health Clinic.
